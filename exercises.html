Exercises

If you truly want to transform your body it does not only matter what exercises you do, but it is also very important to do them right. Here is a list of exercises I do and how to do them:

  • Push Ups
  • Pull Ups
  • Sit Ups
  • Squats

Push Ups

Description: Push your self off the ground with nothing but arms while keeping your body straight.

  • Do at least 5 sets
  • Each set must be at least 10 reps
  • Rest 30 seconds between each set

Pull Ups

Description: Pull your chin over the bar.

  • Do at least 5 sets
  • Each set must be at least 10 reps
  • Rest 30 seconds between each set

Sit Ups

Description: You know how to do this.

  • Do at least 5 sets
  • Each set must be at least 10 reps
  • Rest 30 seconds between each set

Squats

Description: Spread your legs as wide as your shoulders and spread your feet a little(don't make them completly paraller). Lower your gluteus to the ground and go back up.

  • Do at least 5 sets
  • Each set must be at least 10 reps
  • Rest 30 seconds between each set
